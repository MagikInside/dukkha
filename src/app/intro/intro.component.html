Here your adventure begins

In a distant land of powerful magic and dangerous monsters, various tribes and factions struggled to survive and prosper. Among them a select few discovered the ability to bend reality to their will. They were known as Mages, grouped into four powerful castes, or traditions.

A few days ago, in your small town there was a great commotion, when you awakened to the Magic. But the celebrations ended quickly and uneasily. The surrounding dark creatures could also notice the disruption in the spheres and the spike in quintessence concentration.

You were in danger.
